What is the Kabuki-chō

Kabukichou is an entertainment district in Shinjuku,Tokyo,Japan.Kabuki-chō is the location of many host and hostess clubs, love hotels, shops, restaurants, and nightclubs, and is often called the "Sleepless Town" (眠らない街, Nemuranai Machi)
